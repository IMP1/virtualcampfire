About

The Virtual Campfire was created by Leo Murray and Jack Freedman in 2006 as a place for members of the Forest School Camps community to share traditional music. All songs are sung by FSC staff and our friends, recorded by us around our campfires and living rooms as part of our musical tradition. They are for sharing freely and must not be reproduced for financial gain. Fergus took over the website in 2009 - please email him with any feedback.
